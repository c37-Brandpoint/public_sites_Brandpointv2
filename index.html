About AI Visibility Quality Score (VQS)

The AI Visibility Quality Score measures how effectively a brand appears in AI-generated responses. It is a weighted average of five components: Keyword Relevance (15%), Position & Prominence (25%), Share of Voice (20%), Intent Fulfillment (25%), and AI Quality Evaluation (15%).

Each brand has two report tiers: AI Optimize (campaign-level with gated sections and BrandpointIQ upgrade prompts) and BrandpointIQ (fully unlocked with competitor details and complete keyword visibility).
